Remediation Technology

GSI has successfully completed soil and groundwater remediation in a wide range of subsurface environments at numerous locations worldwide. Remedial technologies successfully implemented by GSI include; conventional pump and treat; dual phase extraction; bioremediation; vapor extraction; air-sparging; chemical oxidation; phytoremediation; and many others. In addition to full scale fixed installations, GSI has designed, constructed and maintains a large fleet of state of the art, mobile remediation systems. These systems are configured for multiple remedial and treatment technologies utilizing PLC based controls, surveillance cameras, and telemetry for continuous, remote monitoring and control.
