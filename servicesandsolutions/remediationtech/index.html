Remediation Technology

GSI has successfully completed soil and groundwater remediations in a wide range of subsuface environments at numerous locations world wide. Remedial technologies successfully implemented by GSI include; conventional pump and treat; dual phase extraction; bioremediation; vapor extraction; air-sparging; chemical oxidation; phytoremediation; and many others. In addition to full scale fixed installations, GSI has designed, contructed and maintains a large fleet of state of the art, mobile remediations systems. These systems are configured for multiple remedial and treatment technologies utilizing PLC based controls, surveillance cameras, and telelmetry for continuous, remote monitoring and control.
